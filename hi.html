KOLA PROJECT

HI!

KOLA PROJECT is a start to raise awareness and education on the climate crisis that is happening all around us. Climate change is a hot topic today. Obviously, we all want to protect our planet earth, but how many of us are actively working on it? Sadly, we have found out that not a lot.


We believe that protecting our planet starts with being knowledgeable about what is destroying it. What kills the Earth can go from something as small as throwing that plastic bag into your trashcan to something as massive as the ongoing operations of the neighborhood fossil fuel plant.


Of course, we can't stop the fuel plant, at least not yet. But we can stop throwing plastic in the trash, right?


We hope that resources at KOLA PROJECT will help you educate yourselves, your families, friends, and, most importantly, your children. This could be the start of us taking that first step towards an environmentally ethical future. A future where we can live guilt-free knowing that we are not destroying our only home, the planet Earth.
